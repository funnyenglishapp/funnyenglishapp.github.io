Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your information and protects your privacy. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Online Data Sync

To keep your vocabulary fresh and provide you with new words without needing to update the application, our app periodically connects to our secure servers to check for new word lists. This process is completely anonymous and does not send any of your personal data from your device. It only downloads new vocabulary data to enhance your learning experience.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location. All app settings, preferences, and flashcard progress remain on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share any of your data with anyone. All your data, including your app settings and flashcard progress, is stored securely only on your own device. We, the company, do not have access to it, and we do not share it with any third parties.

Security of Your Data

We take the security of your data seriously.

  • Local Storage: All your app-related data is stored exclusively on your device’s local storage. This means it never leaves your phone, providing a strong layer of privacy.
  • Standard Security Practices: We build our app using standard, commercially reasonable security practices to protect the app’s integrity and the data stored within it.
  • Device Responsibility: However, please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While your data is not transmitted over the internet by our app, it is still only as secure as your device itself. We recommend keeping your device updated and protected with a screen lock or password.

Your Rights and Data Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of it. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or by clearing the app’s storage from your device settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is designed for a general audience and can be safely used by people of all ages, including children. We have a strict policy of not collecting any personally identifiable information. Since we do not ask for names, emails, or any other personal data, we do not knowingly collect such information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and have any concerns, please know that your child can use this app with complete privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We may also provide a prominent notice within the app prior to the change becoming effective and update the “Last updated” date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Compliance

We aim to comply with applicable data protection and privacy laws, to the extent they apply to our Service, including regulations related to the protection of children’s privacy and user data.
