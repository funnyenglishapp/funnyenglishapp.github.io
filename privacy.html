Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how our application, Funny English Stories - Vocab, handles your information and protects your privacy. By using our application, you signify your agreement to the practices described in this comprehensive policy. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection and transparency.

1. Content Retrieval & Architecture

To ensure our users always have access to the most current and relevant educational content, our application utilizes advanced content retrieval systems. These systems periodically synchronize the internal vocabulary database with secure, industry-standard cloud endpoints.

  • Encrypted Synchronisation: All content data is retrieved through modern encrypted protocols to maintain the absolute integrity and authenticity of the educational material.
  • Total Anonymity: This retrieval process is strictly a one-way communication designed for content delivery. We do not transmit any unique device identifiers, hardware serials, or user-specific telemetry to our retrieval servers.

2. Interactive Audio Analysis

A core feature of our application is the 'Voice Game,' which provides real-time feedback on English pronunciation. This feature is built using advanced native system technologies to prioritize user privacy.

  • Volatile Memory Processing: Your voice interactions are processed instantly within the volatile memory (RAM) of your device. This process leverages the device's native operating system engine for immediate analysis.
  • Non-Persistent Architecture: We have a strict policy against audio storage. We do not record, intercept, or upload your voice data to any external server. All analysis occurs within a secure, sandboxed environment and is discarded immediately after providing feedback.

3. Data Collection & Privacy Principles

We operate on a foundational principle of Zero-Data Collection. Our business model is based on education, not data harvesting.

  • No PII Acquisition: We do not collect, request, or store any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). This includes, but is not limited to, names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical locations.
  • No Behavioral Tracking: We do not deploy any third-party analytics or behavioral tracking scripts. Your learning habits, mistake patterns, and progress are entirely private and remain on your device.

4. Localized Information Management

All application preferences, user settings, and learning progress are managed exclusively within the isolated local storage of your specific device.

  • Zero Centralization: Chose data is never centralized. Consequently, it is technically impossible for us to share, sell, or distribute your information to third-party advertisers or data brokers.
  • Absolute User Ownership: You retain complete control over your data. Clearing the application's cache or performing a standard uninstallation will permanently purge all localized information from your device.

5. System Security & Integrity

We employ commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard the application's environment. The application operates within a secure system sandbox, preventing unauthorized access to other files or sensitive data on your device. We recommend keeping your system firmware updated to ensure you have the latest security patches provided by your device manufacturer.

6. Children’s Privacy Compliance

Our application is designed for a general audience and places paramount importance on the safety of younger learners. Our "Privacy-by-Design" approach ensures that no data is ever requested from children, making the application fully compliant with international privacy standards, including the principles of COPPA and GDPR.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to refine this Privacy Policy to reflect updates in our security protocols or retrieval logic. Any significant changes will be communicated by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage users to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.
