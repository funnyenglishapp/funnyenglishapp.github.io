Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies on the collection, use, and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service and tells You about Your privacy rights. By using the Service, You agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Types of Data We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect (Currently)

We want to be crystal clear: Our Service does not require you to create an account. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) like your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or location. Your personal identity is completely anonymous to us.

Information We Collect to Improve Your Experience

To make the app work and improve it, we collect the following non-personal information, which is stored only on your device:

  • Application Settings Data: We save your preferences, such as your chosen theme (dark/light mode) and the last word you viewed. This helps us provide you with a seamless and personal experience every time you open the app.
  • Flashcard Progress Data: We save which words you have marked as "Remembered" or "Forgotten". This data is essential for our intelligent flashcard system to help you learn effectively.

How We Use Your Data

The non-personal data we collect is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain Our Service: Your flashcard progress and settings data are essential for the app's core features to function as intended.
  • To Improve Our Service: In the future, we may analyze anonymous, aggregated data (for example, which words are most frequently forgotten by all users) to improve our learning algorithm and word lists. This analysis will never be linked to an individual user.

Online Data Sync

To keep your vocabulary fresh and provide you with new words without needing to update the application, our app periodically connects to our secure servers to check for new word lists. This process is completely anonymous and does not send any of your personal data from your device. It only downloads new vocabulary data to enhance your learning experience.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share any of your data with anyone.

It's that simple. All your data, including your app settings and flashcard progress, is stored securely only on your own device. We, the company, do not have access to it, and we do not share it with any third parties.

Security of Your Data

We take the security of your data seriously.

  • Local Storage: As mentioned, all your app-related data is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. This means it never leaves your phone, providing a strong layer of privacy.
  • Standard Security Practices: We build our app using standard, commercially reasonable security practices to protect the app's integrity and the data stored within it.
  • Disclaimer: However, please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While your data is not transmitted over the internet by our app, it is still only as secure as your device itself.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is designed for a general audience and can be safely used by people of all ages, including children.

We have a strict policy of not collecting any personally identifiable information. Since we do not ask for names, emails, or any other personal data, we do not knowingly collect any such information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and have any concerns, please know that your child can use this app with complete privacy.
